Welcome to MarketDays


What We Do

We set up an online market, for you, our users, to advertise and trade your items. We also provide a relaxing enviroment for you to enjoy the conversation and trading.

How It Works

MarketDays has thousands of members online everyday. Using your items in your inventory, you can make offers to anyone in your market to trade. Others can also request to trade your items.

What Members Do

On the last day of the online market session, all the participants must bring their trading items to the physical market location, and make the trade.
